Select the commander decks that you think play well together. Connections (edges) between commanders (nodes) are strengthened or weakened based on user selection. Any edge between two selected nodes is strengthened, and any edge between a selected and unselected node is weakened. Edges between unselected nodes are unchanged, therefore making no selection will not affect edge weights. Options are determined via random selection from within a detected community.
